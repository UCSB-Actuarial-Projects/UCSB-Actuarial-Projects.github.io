Work collaboratively, create new tools and applications, get to know other students and build your team!

How it works

It all starts with having a project in good shape that you want to share under the MIT license. This project can be:

  • a project of your own idea that pertains to actuarial work
  • a class project or even a portion thereof that is useful for data science or actuarial work
  • a class team project, with permission from your professor
  • a result from picking one of the proposed projects
  • a partial result from the solution to a case competition problem

In this space, you can be an author, a reviewer, or a project proposer. Your project may be featured on this website as well. We welcome well written blogs about how you developed a project and what you learned from it.
